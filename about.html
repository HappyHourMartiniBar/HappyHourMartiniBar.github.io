About Us

Happy Hour is so excited to be part of Historic Downtown Gresham and its wonderful community!

After 20 years working in special education, owner Shelley Holland retired from the school district. She had always had a love for entertaining so she took her skills to a local establishment.

After happily bartending for a few years, she decided to set out on her own. She’s not very good at being retired.

Come by for brunch, Happy Hour, date night, late night or for a refreshing cocktail after a bike ride in our upscale mid-century modern atmosphere. With a full bar staffed by skilled and educated bartenders, a menu of comfort food with a twist and an all-day brunch, we say,

“Why limit Happy to an Hour?”
